The Origin
The Spark in Tenali

In the vibrant town of Tenali, Andhra Pradesh, Suresh Manne noticed something powerful — vehicle number plates weren't just digits. They carried stories of pride, luck, and identity.

That observation sparked a question: "What if people could have 10-digit mobile numbers just as unique and meaningful?" That single insight became the cornerstone of Manne Communications.

From a single idea in 2015, we've served over 10,000 satisfied customers across every major Indian city — from businessmen and celebrities to students seeking their perfect numerology number.
