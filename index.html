The Origin
The Spark in Tenali
In the vibrant town of Tenali, Andhra Pradesh, Suresh Manne noticed something powerful — vehicle number plates weren't just random digits. They carried stories of pride, luck, and identity. People paid premium prices for plates that meant something to them.
That observation sparked a question: "What if people could have 10-digit mobile numbers just as unique and meaningful?" That single insight became the cornerstone of Manne Communications.
From a single idea in 2015, we've served over 10,000 satisfied customers across every major Indian city — from businessmen and celebrities to students and homemakers, all seeking that perfect number.
