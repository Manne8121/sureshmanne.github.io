🛒 Buying Process
How do I buy a VIP number from Manne Communications?
It's simple: Browse our collection → Click Interested or Buy Now → Confirm with Mr. Suresh on WhatsApp → Make payment → Receive UPC code within 24 hours → Visit any retailer with UPC and ID proof → Number activated in your name in 3–7 working days.
What is a UPC code and how does it work?
UPC (Unique Porting Code) is an 8-digit code that allows you to port (transfer) a number to yourself through the official MNP process. Once you receive the UPC from Mr. Suresh, visit any retailer or operator showroom, submit the UPC with your ID proof, and the number gets activated in your name within 3 to 7 working days.
How long is the UPC valid?
Most of India — UPC is valid for 4 days from the date of issue. North East India and Jammu & Kashmir — UPC is valid for 30 days. Complete the activation process within this period.
Can I get the number activated from anywhere in India?
Yes. You can activate your VIP number at any retailer store or operator showroom across India. We provide All India delivery and the number can be activated in your city regardless of where it was originally issued.
What happens at the retailer during activation?
The retailer will capture your photograph twice, verify your Aadhaar or valid government document, and complete live biometric verification (fingerprint or iris). For corporate activations — company official letterhead with authorised signatory must be provided. This is a standard TRAI and DOT mandated process for all SIM activations in India.
📋 Legal & Safety
Are your numbers 100% legal?
Absolutely. Every number we sell is fully compliant with TRAI, DOT and Telecom Operator guidelines. We strictly follow all government and regulatory rules so your purchase is completely hassle-free and legally sound.
Will the number be in my name?
Yes — always. Every number we sell or service we provide will be activated in your name or your company's name with proper KYC documentation. We never sell numbers activated in someone else's name.
Do you sell pre-activated SIM cards?
No. We strictly do not sell pre-activated SIMs. Every number is freshly activated through the official MNP process in the buyer's name with valid ID proof. This ensures complete legal compliance and safety for the customer.
Do you sell used numbers?
No. We only deal in numbers available through official telecom channels. We do not sell numbers previously used in financial fraud, scams, spam or any illegal activity.
📱 Number Types & Plans
Do you provide prepaid numbers?
Yes. We provide VIP and fancy prepaid numbers across all major operators — Airtel, Jio, Vi (Vodafone Idea) and BSNL. The number will be ported and activated as a fresh prepaid connection in your name.
Do you provide postpaid numbers?
Yes. We provide premium postpaid VIP numbers for individuals and corporates. Postpaid activation requires standard operator documentation and address proof in addition to ID proof.
Do you provide CUG (Closed User Group) numbers?
Yes. We provide CUG number series for offices, teams and organisations. CUG plans allow free or discounted calling within a group. Contact Mr. Suresh with your group size and requirements.
Do you provide group or consecutive number series?
Yes. We provide consecutive and matching number series for families, businesses and organisations. WhatsApp Mr. Suresh with your requirement.
Do you provide international roaming numbers?
Yes. We provide international roaming SIM cards and travel assistance for 50+ countries including USA, UK, UAE, Australia, Singapore, Canada and more. WhatsApp Mr. Suresh before your trip and we'll sort your SIM and number before you board.
📄 Documents Required
What documents are needed to buy a SIM card?
We strictly require original documents for all activations. For Individuals: Aadhaar Card (mandatory — live biometric verification required) or Passport, Voter ID, Driving Licence, PAN Card as approved by the telecom operator. For Corporate: Company official letterhead with authorised signatory, GST certificate or company registration document, authorised person's Aadhaar and photo ID, live biometric of the authorised representative. Note: Documents are only required at the retailer during activation — we at Manne Communications do NOT ask for any documents.
Do you ask for Aadhaar or personal documents?
No. Mr. Suresh does not ask for your Aadhaar or any personal documents at any stage of the purchase. Documents are only required at the retailer or showroom during SIM activation — that process is between you and the telecom operator directly. We only need your Date of Birth for numerology matching — nothing else.
Do you store my personal data?
No. We do not store any of your personal data. All conversations happen on WhatsApp directly with Mr. Suresh and are strictly confidential. We do not share your information with any third party under any circumstances.
