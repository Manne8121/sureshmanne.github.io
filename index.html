Your VIP number can be ported to any operator — Jio, Airtel, Vi (Vodafone-Idea), or BSNL — in any state across India. The porting process uses the standard MNP (Mobile Number Portability) procedure. Once you receive the UPC code from Mr. Suresh, visit any retailer of your preferred network, show your UPC + Aadhaar, and your number gets activated within 3–7 working days. The number remains yours — only the operator changes.
